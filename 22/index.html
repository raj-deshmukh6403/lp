Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education contributing to the development of computing technology and enterprise competence to meet the socio-economic challenges of the modern world.

Mission

  • To develop competent computing professionals with social sensitivity
  • To promote research and innovation in collaboration with industry and society
  • To provide an environment for continuous learning and professional growth

Our History

Established in 1983, PICT has grown into one of the premier technical institutes in Maharashtra. The institute is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University and is approved by AICTE.

Achievements

Academic Excellence

PICT has been consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in the state with exceptional academic results.

Research & Innovation

Our faculty and students have published numerous research papers in international journals and conferences.

Industry Collaboration

PICT has strong ties with leading tech companies, facilitating excellent placement opportunities for students.

Computer Engineering

The Department of Computer Engineering at PICT provides comprehensive education in various aspects of computer science and engineering.

Key Research Areas:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Data Science & Big Data Analytics
  • Cloud Computing & Distributed Systems
  • Computer Networks & Security

Faculty Strength: 40

Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology focuses on software development, database management, and IT infrastructure.

Key Research Areas:

  • Software Engineering & Architecture
  • Web & Mobile Application Development
  • Database Systems & Data Mining
  • Internet of Things (IoT)

Faculty Strength: 35

Electronics & Telecommunication

The E&TC department provides education in electronic circuits, communication systems, and signal processing.

Key Research Areas:

  • VLSI Design & Embedded Systems
  • Wireless Communication
  • Signal & Image Processing
  • Antenna Design & RF Engineering

Faculty Strength: 30

Basic Sciences & Humanities

This department handles foundation courses in Mathematics, Physics, and Humanities for first-year engineering students.

Key Areas:

  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Applied Physics
  • Communication Skills
  • Professional Ethics

Faculty Strength: 25
